The Platform

Four pillars. One complete system.

Every module is designed with structure, clarity, and real-world application so teens can build confidence that sticks.

💪

Physical Coordination & Strength

Exercises that improve posture, coordination, balance, and body awareness through motion-based video modules.

  • 5 series, 10 exercise modules
  • Quiz at end of each series
  • Motion video exercises
  • Honor-system completion tracking
🏠

Independence Skills

Lessons that teach real-world abilities such as cooking, budgeting, organization, and daily living routines.

  • 4 series, 8 real-world skill modules
  • Weekly challenge (cook a meal, build a budget)
  • Photo or written reflection with AI feedback
  • Quiz at the end of each series
💬

Social Skills

Interactive training focused on communication, conversation flow, emotional awareness, and navigating social situations.

  • 5 series, 10 scenario-based models
  • Role-play situations with guided prompts
  • AI-graded reflection journal
  • Self-scored confidence tracking (1–10)
📋

Executive Function & Daily Structure

Tools and lessons designed to improve planning, task management, focus, and organization for daily life.

  • 4 series, 8 planning & focus modules
  • Daily structure builder each week
  • Task breakdown challenges with deadlines
  • Completion tracking via progress dashboard
